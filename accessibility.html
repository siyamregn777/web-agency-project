Accessibility Statement

Nexify is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continuously improve the user experience for everyone and apply relevant accessibility standards.

Our commitment

Nexify strives to make its website and digital services accessible to all people, regardless of technology or ability. We are actively working to increase accessibility and usability of our website to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 level AA.

Compliance status

This website is partially compliant with the Swedish accessibility regulations (Lagen om tillgänglighet till digital offentlig service). We have designed and tested internal pages to meet WCAG 2.1 AA, with ongoing audits and remediation.

  • ✔️ Text alternatives for non-text content
  • ✔️ Keyboard accessible navigation & focus indicators
  • ✔️ Sufficient color contrast (minimum 4.5:1)
  • ✔️ Semantic HTML structure & ARIA landmarks
  • ✔️ Responsive zoom without horizontal scroll
  • 🔄 Ongoing: third-party integrations testing

Technical information

This website is designed to be compatible with assistive technologies including screen readers (NVDA, VoiceOver, TalkBack) and browser zoom up to 200%. We use:

  • HTML5 & CSS3 with responsive design
  • JavaScript for interactive components (hamburger menu) with ARIA attributes
  • No reliance on hover-only interactions
  • Focus management and skip-to-content links

If you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to report the matter to the Swedish Agency for Digital Government (DIGG). We aim to resolve accessibility issues promptly and transparently.
